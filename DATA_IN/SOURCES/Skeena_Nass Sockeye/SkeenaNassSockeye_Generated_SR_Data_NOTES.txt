SkeenaNassSockeye_Generated_SR_Data.csv

This file is copied from the Skeena and Nass simulation model input folder.
It contains cleaned and processed SR data by stock.

- Most Skeena and Nass stocks line up with CUs
- Spawner estimates are generally based on index stream escapements that are scaled up.

These data have:
- filtered out brood years with unlikely numbers (RpS>45)
- infilled where possible
